English Communication

English improves through listening, reading, speaking, and writing. The goal is not only correct grammar but also clear communication and confidence.

Vocabulary

Learn words in context. Instead of memorizing a list, use each new word in a sentence of your own.

Reading

Read short passages and ask: What is the main idea? Which words show the answer?

Writing

Good writing uses simple sentences, correct punctuation, and a clear order of ideas.

How To Improve Speaking

  • Read aloud for a few minutes every day.
  • Practice introducing yourself clearly and politely.
  • Use short and correct sentences before trying difficult ones.
  • Listen to good English and repeat the pronunciation.

Writing Better Answers

  • Start with a direct answer to the question.
  • Add one or two supporting points or examples.
  • Use linking words like because, however, first, and finally.
  • Always recheck spelling and punctuation.

Daily Practice Tip

Keep a small notebook for new words, useful phrases, and short writing practice. Small daily effort creates steady progress.
